# GTD Email Categories Reference

## GTD/Urgent (Dark Red)
- Production outages
- Executive escalations
- Time-sensitive deadlines (today)
- Critical incidents
- **Always wins over other categories**

## GTD/Action (Orange)
- Tasks to complete
- Decisions to make
- Documents to review
- Meetings to schedule
- **User must DO something**

## GTD/Reply (Yellow)
- Questions to answer
- Feedback requests
- Availability confirmations
- **Written response only, no other action**

## GTD/Waiting (Green)
- Delegated tasks
- Pending approvals
- Awaiting responses
- **Tracking someone else's action**

## GTD/Review (Purple)
- FYI emails
- Status updates
- Newsletters
- Announcements
- **Read/digest, no action required**

## GTD/Ignore (Gray)
- Automated notifications
- Marketing emails
- Irrelevant CC'd threads
- **Safe to skip or archive**
